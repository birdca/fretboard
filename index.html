Fretboard

HTML/SVG Scale Charts & Chord Diagrams for Guitar, Ukulele, and similar instruments. In development, coming very soon. For some thoughts so far, check out my brief writeup.

The order I'm tackling things is as follows. Once I get all this done, I'll work on exciting ways to package together the product and build a compelling user experience for referencing the chords and scales that are often sought after.

  1. Guitar chord diagrams (vertical fretboard, various sizes)
  2. Ukulele chord diagrams (vertical fretboard, various sizes)
  3. Guitar scale diagrams (horizontal fretboard, various sizes)
  4. Ukulele scale diagrams (horizontal fretboard, various sizes)
